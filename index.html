You have 5 turns. Each turn you get a blue meteor except for turn 3 you get a yellow Meteor , and you have a board with 9 tiles . Each tile has 3 HP except for the middle tile, it has 4 HP. You have to drag and drop a random number of blue meteors, between 3-5, each turn, while on turn 3 you only have to drag and drop 1 yellow meteor. The blue meteor deals 1 dmg, while the yellow one deals 3 dmg, and the yellow meteor has a huge range. The meteor range is indicated by the yellow circle around it, so BE CAREFUL IF YOU DROPPED A METEOR BETWEEN TILES, IT WILL HIT ALL THE TILES IN BETWEEN. Your job is to finish all 5 turns without destroying more than 3 tiles, or destroy the middle tile. If all turns end without doing so YOU WIN. If you destroyed 4 or more tiles you lose, and if the middle tile got destroyed YOU LOSE.
