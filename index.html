Meteors Drag & Drop Mini Game

WELCOME to my Meteors mini mini Drag & Drop game

This game is my personal project, as an implmentation for what I have learned so far about HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

Made by: Ahmed Saeed

List of rules:

  1. You have 5 turns. Each turn you get a blue meteor except for turn 3 you get a yellow one.
  2. You have to drop a random number, between 2-4, of blue meteors each turn, while at turn 3, you only have to drop 1 yellow meteor.
  3. You have a board with 9 tiles. Each tile has 3 HP except for the middle tile, it has 4 HP.
  4. The blue meteor deals 1 dmg, while the yellow one deals 3 dmg, and the yellow meteor has a huge range
  5. Drag and Drop a meteor inside any tile, BUT BE CAREFUL IF YOU DROPPED IT BETWEEN TILES, IT WILL HIT ALL THE TILES IN BETWEEN.
  6. Your job is to finish all 5 turns without destroying more than 3 tiles, and to keep the middle tile alive to WIN.
  7. If you destroyed 4 or more tiles you lose, and If the middle tile got destroyed YOU LOSE.
